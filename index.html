Why Choose a Specialist?

THE BENEFITS OF CHOOSING AN OCULOPLASTIC SURGEON

The eyes are often the first feature someone sees when they look at you, so when treating a cosmetic and/or functional concern in or around the eye region, it is imperative that you choose the right physician. While many plastic and cosmetic surgeons offer surgical options for the eyelids and surrounding areas, it is unlikely they have dedicated their medical training—and practices as a whole—exclusively to the treatment of periocular issues.

Contrarily, oculoplastic surgeons have undergone advanced training in the subspecialty of ophthalmic plastic and reconstructive surgery, which combines plastic surgery and ophthalmology to treat diseases and conditions affecting the eyelids, eyebrows, tear duct system, the bone around the eye, and the adjacent face. By selecting a skilled and experienced oculoplastic surgeon like Dr. Connor for your periocular needs, you can rest assured you are under the care of a physician who has devoted their medical career to the very specialty you seek.
